OUR FREEDOM OUR RESPONSIBILITY

teaching young Indian the importance of being involved in our democracy and how to be effective.

OUR BELIEFS

The lack of citizen involvement is a quiet epidemic and a threat to our future. Indian need a broader view of citizenship – it goes well beyond voting, jury duty, and community service. Effective citizens do simple, yet impactful things – citizen actions – on a daily basis that keep the country free.
“It is easy to kill individuals but you cannot kill the Ideas. Great empires crumbled, while the ideas survived.” –Bhagat Singh
CITIZEN ACTION

We have identified 100 citizen actions, explained why they matter, and grouped them into 6 categories:

Heritage: – Actions that remind us of our heritage.
Democratic Process:– Actions that involve us in the democratic process.
Special Group:Actions that support special groups with a responsibility for keeping the country strong.
Government: – Actions that help us understand our government.
Community:Actions that promote a sense of community.
Indivisuals & Family:– Actions that strengthen individuals and families.
